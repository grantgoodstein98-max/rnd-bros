Formulation Expertise

Ingredients we've mastered

Ashwagandha (KSM-66® & Sensoril®) +
Adaptogen
Bitter masking solved — our gummies taste clean, not earthy.
Ashwagandha is an adaptogenic herb used to help the body manage stress and regulate cortisol levels. It supports a calm, balanced mood and has been studied for its effects on anxiety reduction and physical endurance. KSM-66® and Sensoril® are the two most clinically validated extracts on the market.
🍄
Lion's Mane Extract +
Functional Mushroom
Stability-tested for active hericenone retention through shelf life.
Lion's Mane is a functional mushroom prized for its ability to support cognitive function, memory, and focus. It contains hericenones and erinacines — compounds studied for their role in stimulating nerve growth factor (NGF), which supports brain plasticity and long-term neurological health.
🌸
Rhodiola Rosea +
Adaptogen
Standardized to 3% rosavins — we source and verify every lot.
Rhodiola Rosea is an adaptogen traditionally used to combat fatigue, improve mental performance under stress, and support mood stability. Its active compounds, rosavins and salidroside, have been studied for their effects on physical endurance and resilience to burnout.
🧠
L-Theanine +
Amino Acid
Pairs cleanly with ashwagandha for a natural calm + focus stack.
L-Theanine is an amino acid found naturally in green tea that promotes relaxed alertness without causing drowsiness. It works synergistically with other actives to take the edge off stimulants and support a calm, focused mental state — making it a staple in stress and cognitive support formulations.
💤
Melatonin +
Sleep Support
Precise micro-dosing (0.5mg–5mg) with validated label accuracy.
Melatonin is the body's primary sleep-regulating hormone, signaling the brain that it's time to wind down. In supplement form it's used to support sleep onset, reduce jet lag, and improve overall sleep quality. Precise dosing is critical — effective doses range from 0.5mg to 5mg and label accuracy must be verified by COA.
💊
Magnesium Glycinate +
Mineral
High-absorption form without the texture compromise.
Magnesium Glycinate is a highly bioavailable form of magnesium — bonded to the amino acid glycine for superior absorption and minimal digestive side effects. It supports muscle relaxation, quality sleep, and nervous system function, and is widely used in recovery, stress, and sleep-focused formulations.
⚡
B-Vitamin Complex +
Energy Support
Color-stable formulations that won't turn your gummy brown.
B-Vitamins (B6, B9, B12 and others) are essential co-factors in cellular energy metabolism, red blood cell production, and neurological function. They're among the most popular actives in energy and wellness gummies. The formulation challenge is color stability — high-dose B-vitamins can discolor gummies over time, which we've solved.

FAQ

Questions founders always ask

What's your minimum order quantity? +

Pilot runs start from 100 units — designed for founders who want to validate before they scale. That said, MOQ and timeline both depend on formula complexity. A simple vitamin gummy can ship in as little as 8 weeks. A novel adaptogen blend with custom flavor masking will take longer. Your quote will include a formula-specific MOQ and timeline before you commit to anything.

What does 'third-party tested' mean for my product? +

Every production lot receives a Certificate of Analysis (COA) from an independent laboratory — confirming potency, purity, and the absence of contaminants. You receive this document and can share it directly with your customers or retail buyers. It's the foundation of a clean label claim.

Can I use an ingredient that isn't on your list? +

Almost certainly yes. We work with novel, emerging, and proprietary compounds regularly — not just the mainstream actives. If you have an unusual ingredient, a proprietary extract, or something you've not seen in a gummy before, bring it to us. We'll assess feasibility as part of your discovery call.

What health claims can I make about adaptogens or functional ingredients? +

Adaptogen and functional supplement products can use structure/function claims — for example, 'supports a healthy stress response' or 'promotes mental clarity.' These require the standard FDA disclaimer. We review your intended label claims as part of our documentation package so you go to market clean.

Can I get NSF, Non-GMO, Organic, or Kosher certification? +

We support certification pathways for NSF, Non-GMO Project, Organic, Kosher, and Halal. Each has its own timeline and cost, which we'll map out specifically for your product during the discovery process.

How many prototype rounds do I get? +

As many as it takes. Prototype rounds continue until you approve the formula. We don't charge extra for revisions — it's built into your project quote from day one.
